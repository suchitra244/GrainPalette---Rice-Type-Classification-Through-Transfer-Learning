Welcome to Rice Grain Classification

This application classifies rice grains into different categories based on their shape, size, and texture.

Rice Grain Types

Arborio

Arborio rice is a short-grain rice known for its creamy texture, often used in risottos.

Basmati

Basmati rice is a long-grain aromatic rice widely used in Indian and Middle Eastern cuisines.

Ipsala

Ipsala rice is a medium-grain variety grown in Turkey, known for its versatility in dishes.

Jasmine

Jasmine rice is a fragrant long-grain rice, popular in Thai cuisine due to its floral aroma.

Karacadag

Karacadag rice is a specialty rice from Turkey, known for its unique taste and texture.

Popular Rice Types in India

Sona Masoori

A lightweight, aromatic rice variety primarily grown in Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka.

Ponni

Ponni rice is a popular South Indian variety, known for its soft texture and quick cooking properties.

Black Rice

Also known as 'forbidden rice', it is rich in antioxidants and primarily cultivated in Manipur.

Matta Rice

A bold and nutritious rice variety commonly used in Kerala for traditional meals.
