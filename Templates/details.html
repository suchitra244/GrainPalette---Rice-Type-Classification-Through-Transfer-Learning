Rice Grain Categories

  • Arborio: Short-grain, round, used in risotto.
  • Basmati: Long-grain, aromatic, used in biryanis.
  • Ipsala: Medium-grain, grown in Turkey.
  • Jasmine: Fragrant, long-grain, used in Thai cuisine.
  • Karacadag: Specialty rice from Turkey.
